# Sharing Libraries with Friends and Family

Phlex Hub allows you to share your media libraries with other Hub users. This is perfect for sharing your movie collection with a partner or letting your children access your TV shows.

## How Library Sharing Works

When you share a library:
- The share is at the **library level**, not the server level — you can share just your "Movies" library without sharing your entire server
- You control the **permission level**: read-only or read+write
- Shares can be set to **expire** automatically (optional)
- You can **revoke** shares at any time
- The person you share with sees the library in their "Shared with Me" section

## Sharing a Library

1. Go to **My Servers** and select a server
2. Find the library you want to share
3. Click the **Share** button
4. Enter the email address of the person you want to share with
5. Choose the permission level:
   - **Read only** — they can browse and play content but not modify
   - **Read/Write** — they can also modify metadata, create playlists, etc.
6. Optionally set an expiration date
7. Click **Share**

The person will receive the library in their "Shared with Me" section.

## Managing Your Shares

Go to **Manage Shares** to see all libraries you've shared with others. From here you can:
- See who each library is shared with
- Change the permission level
- Revoke a share

## Shared Libraries Dashboard

When someone shares a library with you, you'll see it in the **Shared with Me** section of your dashboard. Click on any shared library to browse it — you'll access it using your Hub credentials, no separate login required.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I share an entire server?**
Not directly — shares are per-library. You can share as many libraries as you like to achieve the same effect.

**What happens when a share expires?**
The library will disappear from the collaborator's "Shared with Me" section automatically.

**Can I share with someone who doesn't have a Hub account?**
For now, sharing requires both users to have Hub accounts. We're working on invite links for future versions.

**Can I see what my shared libraries look like on the server?**
Yes — shared libraries appear exactly like your own libraries on your server dashboard.

**Who can I share with?**
Any registered Hub user. If they don't have an account yet, they'll need to sign up first.
